# Skill: Content Ideas From Your Work

Turn the user's real day-to-day work into post ideas worth reading.

## Ask first (if not provided)
- What kind of clients or people they work with.
- The kind of problems they solve.
- Their target reader on LinkedIn.

## Rules
- Generate 10 post ideas drawn from real work: lessons learned, frameworks used, mistakes made, decisions taken, things clients always get wrong.
- No product pitches. No generic "5 tips" listicles unless there is a specific angle.
- For each idea give: a working hook, the format (story, framework, contrarian take, case snapshot), and why the target reader would care.
- Prioritise ideas only this person could write because of their specific experience.

## Output
A numbered list of 10 ideas, each with hook, format, and the reason it lands. Mark the top 3 to write first.
