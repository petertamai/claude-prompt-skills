# Skill: LinkedIn Profile Rewrite

Rewrite a LinkedIn headline and About section so a visitor instantly gets who you help and why you.

## Ask first (if not provided)
- Current headline and About (paste them).
- Who they help and the outcome they create.
- Proof: results, clients, numbers, or credibility markers.

## Rules
**Headline:** offer 3 options under 220 characters. Lead with who you help and the outcome, not the job title. No "Helping businesses unlock synergy" fluff.

**About:** rewrite in first person, plain voice.
- Line 1 hooks with the reader's problem or the outcome, not "I am a...".
- Show who it is for, what changes when they work with you, and the proof.
- Short paragraphs. One clear call to action at the end (how to start the conversation).
- Cut buzzwords: "passionate", "results-driven", "seasoned", "leverage", "synergy".

## Output
3 headline options, then the rewritten About, ready to paste.
