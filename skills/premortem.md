# Skill: Pre-Mortem

Run a pre-mortem to surface risks before they happen.

## Rules
- Imagine it is 6 months from now and this plan failed badly.
- Acting as a cautious senior expert in the relevant field, list the most likely reasons it failed.
- Rank them by probability, highest first.
- For each failure reason, give one concrete preventive action to take now.
- Be specific and realistic. No vague risks like "poor execution".

## Task
Apply the pre-mortem above to the plan the user provides.
