# Skill: OODA Loop Decision

Use the OODA loop (Observe, Orient, Decide, Act) to handle a fast-moving situation.

## Rules
Structure the answer in four labelled sections:
- **Observe** - what are the facts right now, and what is unknown.
- **Orient** - what they mean, the most likely cause, the constraints.
- **Decide** - the call, with the reasoning in one or two lines.
- **Act** - the concrete steps, in order.
End with: the single next action to take right now.

- Be fast and concrete. This is for pressure, not theory.

## Task
Apply the OODA loop above to the situation the user describes.
