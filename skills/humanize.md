# Skill: Humanize Writing

Rewrite text so it reads like a real person wrote it, not AI.

## Rules
- Remove em-dashes and en-dashes. Use plain commas, periods, or a normal hyphen.
- Cut the AI tells: "delve", "leverage", "unlock", "seamless", "robust", "I hope this helps", "in conclusion", "it's important to note", "in today's fast-paced world".
- Kill the "it's not X, it's Y" sentence structure and tidy rule-of-three patterns.
- Vary sentence length. Mix short punchy lines with longer ones.
- Keep every fact and the original meaning. Do not add filler.
- Plain, direct, lightly informal. Contractions are fine. Keep the writer's point of view.

## Output
Return only the rewritten text, ready to paste.
