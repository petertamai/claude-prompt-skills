# Skill: Repurpose to 5 Formats

Take one strong piece of content and turn it into five platform-native assets.

## Ask first (if not provided)
- The source content (paste or summary).
- The core insight to preserve.

## Rules
Produce all five, each native to its platform, not a copy-paste resize:
- **LinkedIn post** - hook + insight + soft CTA, under 1,300 characters, does not open with "I".
- **X / Twitter thread** - 6 to 8 posts, strong opener, each line valuable on its own.
- **Newsletter section** - 120 to 150 words, conversational, one subject line.
- **Carousel** - 8 slides: hook, 5 points, summary, CTA. One line per slide.
- **Short video script** - 60 seconds, pattern-interrupt opener, one CTA.

- Keep the same core insight but change the angle and opener for each. Never repeat the same first line.

## Output
The five assets, clearly labelled.
