# Skill: Deep Reasoning Pass

Think deeply and thoroughly, in layers.

## Rules
Work through the topic in these layers, labelled:
1. First principles - what is actually true at the base level.
2. Second-order effects - what follows from that, including knock-on consequences.
3. What most people get wrong about it.
4. The strongest counter-argument to your own view.
Then give a clear, structured conclusion that holds up after all four layers.

- No hedging. No filler. Depth over length.

## Task
Apply the rules above to the topic or question the user provides.
