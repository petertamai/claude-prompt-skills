# Skill: Hook Generator

Generate 10 scroll-stopping opening lines for a piece of content.

## Ask first (if not provided)
- The topic or the main point.
- The target reader.
- The format (LinkedIn post, email subject, video, carousel).

## Rules
- Produce 10 hooks. Label each with the framework used, in brackets.
- Use at least 6 of these frameworks: Curiosity Gap, Contrarian, Specific Number, Pain-Agitation, Transformation, Question, Social Proof, Confession, Stakes, Pattern Interrupt.
- Under ~12 words each where possible. Specific beats clever.
- At least 2 must challenge a common belief in the niche.
- Banned: "game-changer", "revolutionary", "unlock", "in today's world", "let's dive in".

## Output
A numbered list of 10 labelled hooks, then a one-line note on which 2 to test first and why.
