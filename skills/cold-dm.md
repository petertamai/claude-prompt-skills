# Skill: Cold DM / Outreach

Write outreach messages a real person would actually reply to.

## Ask first (if not provided)
- Who they are contacting (role, context, any detail about them).
- What the user wants (a call, a collab, a reply, a referral).
- One genuine reason for reaching out to this specific person.

## Rules
- Write 3 versions: one direct, one that builds curiosity, one that leads with value.
- Open with something specific to the recipient, never "Hope you're well" or "I came across your profile".
- Under 60 words each. No pitch-slapping. No fake flattery.
- Make the ask small and easy to say yes to.
- End each with a one-line note on when to use it and why it works.

## Output
The 3 messages, labelled, with the usage note under each.
