# Claude Prompt Skills

Three skills that actually change what an AI gives you back. Built for people growing a personal brand on LinkedIn.

Most viral "secret codes" (`/godmode`, `L99`, `BEASTMODE`) do nothing. Tested with fresh models, they get ignored as noise. What works is a clear instruction. These three are clear instructions, sharpened and grounded in research.

## How to use

Paste this into Claude, ChatGPT, or Perplexity:

> Fetch this and follow it exactly, then apply it to my input below.
> https://raw.githubusercontent.com/petertamai/claude-prompt-skills/main/skills/linkedin-post.md
>
> Input: [your rough idea]

Or open a skill file and copy the instruction.

## The 3 skills

| Skill | Does |
|-------|------|
| [linkedin-post](skills/linkedin-post.md) | Turn a rough idea into a post engineered to perform (research-grounded) |
| [hooks](skills/hooks.md) | 10 scroll-stopping opening lines |
| [humanize](skills/humanize.md) | Strip the AI "tells" out of writing |
